VOWEL SOUNDS

Vowel Sounds is the debut album-length offering from Justin Myles Holmes. Recorded in Nashville with a hand-picked ensemble of award-winning bluegrass session musicians, it packs a hard-driving acoustic feel along with the wind instruments and varied singing techniques that have made Holmes' live shows a draw at blockchain events around the world.

So how does this work?

The record is encrypted and available for download. Once contributions have crossed the threshold of 10ETH, the decryption key will be made available. The record will be then be in the public domain, free to download and use under a creative commons license.
