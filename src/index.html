VOWEL SOUNDS

Vowel Sounds is the debut album-length offering from Justin Myles Holmes. Along with a classic piece from Final Fantasy IX arranged for bluegrass, it contains original songs with lyrics addressing surveillance, crypto-economics on the world stage, war profittering, and an optimistic outlook on the future of humanity and technology.
